About Cyber Guardians

Empowering Cyber Minds,

One Byte at a Time.

Cyber Guardians is a student-led cybersecurity community founded at Global Institute of Technology, Jaipur, with the mission to cultivate cyber awareness, encourage ethical hacking, and prepare students for careers in the digital defense landscape.

---------------------------------

In a world where cyber threats are rapidly evolving, Cyber Guardians acts as a bridge between classroom learning and real-world cybersecurity practices. Through hands-on workshops, expert talks, awareness campaigns, Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges, and community-driven research, we aim to foster a strong cybersecurity culture among students.

---------------------------------
