Last Updated: 23/06/2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information, such as login details or preferences, for a better browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following types of cookies on our Site:

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of our Site. They enable you to navigate the Site and use essential features, such as accessing secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our Site, such as which pages are visited most often. This data helps us improve our Site's performance. All information collected by these cookies is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our Site to remember choices you make, such as your language preference or the region you are in, to provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: We may use these cookies to deliver relevant advertisements to you on our Site or other platforms. They remember that you have visited our Site and track your browsing activity.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, such as analytics providers or advertisers, to set cookies on our Site. These cookies are used to track your behavior on other websites, deliver targeted ads, and measure advertising effectiveness. We do not control these cookies, and they are subject to the third party’s privacy policies.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience by remembering preferences and settings;
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns to improve our Site;
  • Provide relevant content and advertisements;
  • Ensure the Site functions securely and efficiently.

5. Managing Cookies

You have options to manage or disable cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Consult your browser’s help section to learn how to do this. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our Site.
  • Opt-Out Tools: You may also opt-out of cookies used for targeted advertising by visiting websites like the Network Advertising Initiative’s Opt-Out page or the Digital Advertising Alliance’s Opt-Out page.
